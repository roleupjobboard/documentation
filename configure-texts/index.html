Change title, subtitle and description

From your dashboard, click on "Configure", then on the "Edit" button of the "Texts and Design" section and on the "Edit" button of the "Texts" section

On this page, you can change your job board title, subtitle and description. The title and the subtitle are plain text but you can style the description with bold and italic styles and you can add links as well.
